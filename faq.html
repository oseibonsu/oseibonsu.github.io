Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1. I don’t know anything. Should I come out and do a social dance?
    Absolutely! Everyone starts as a beginner. The more you challenge yourself, the more fun you’ll have.
  • 2. How do I improve?
    By dancing! Attend lessons, socials, practicas, and practice on your own or with a partner.
  • 3. What’s the best way to make friends?
    Simply talk to people and ask them about their dance journey. You can also invite them to practice together.
  • 4. When should I start learning head movement?
    After your footwork and fundamentals are solid. Head movements build on the basics, so master them first.
