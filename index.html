Welcome to the Austin Zouk & Lambada Community!

We’re thrilled to have you join us! Dancing is a joyful way to connect, make friends, and express yourself.

I cannot stress this enough: beginners are the best thing to happen to any dance community. The more people who join, the more opportunities we have to bring exciting events, learn from each other, and ultimately enjoy dancing together. You are not a “bad” dancer; you are a beginner embarking on an exciting journey!
