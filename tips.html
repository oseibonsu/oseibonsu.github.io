Tips and Suggestions

  • Introduce yourself to others – a simple “Hi, I’m [Name], I’m new here” goes a long way.
  • Don’t be shy to ask someone to dance or practice; it’s a great way to improve and make friends.
  • Stay after class for the social time or chat with fellow dancers.
  • Volunteering at events is a great way to meet people and get involved.
  • Be mindful of personal hygiene. Dress comfortably and keep breath mints or gum handy.
  • Apologize if you bump someone or make a mistake. A quick “Sorry!” helps keep the atmosphere friendly.
